About

Description

  • Mr. Rajesh Kumar Pandey, IPS, Deputy Inspector General of Police, Bareilly Range Bareilly is native of the district Prayagraj (Allahabad).
  • Mr. Rajesh Kumar Pandey passed the examination of M.Sc. in Botany in 1982 from Allahabad University.
  • In the year 1984, After Passing the examination of U.G.C NET,J.R.F.SRF worked as a Research Associate in IARI (Pusa Institute).
  • From U.P.P.S.C. in 1986, Mr. Pandey being elected to the post of Deputy Superintendent of Police, he worked as Circle Police Officer in District Sonbhadra, Jaunpur, Azamgarh and Lucknow.
  • After that he was posted as Senior Superintendent of Police at Rampur, Saharanpur, Gonda, Lucknow, Aligarh and Meerut.
  • Subsequently, in the Security Branch was posted on the post of Deputy Inspector General of Police in Uttar Pradesh, This officer, is one of the Founder Member of U.P. S.T.F (Special Task Force) And A.T.S. (Anti Terrorist Squad ).
  • The surveillance system was started in Uttar Pradesh Police by him, which gave new direction to the unveiling of crimes.
  • During his appointment in U.P. STF And A.T.S., remarkable breakthrough has been achieved in the efficient supervision of Encounter and arrest of many notorious criminals.
  • Shri Rajesh Kumar Pandey received 04 times the Gallantry Award of the Honorable President of India for the outstanding work of the Police Department, 01 time the honorable service medal of the Honorable President of India, and the United Nations Peace Keeping Medal received in Kosovo (U.N).
  • Investigated major incidents of terrorist attack in state of U.P terrorist attack on Ayodhya on 05/07/2005 and of Varanasi 07/03/2006.
  • Experience of counter terrorist operation in the state of U.P
  • During the course of police service, developed experience in the field of electronic surveillance particularly of mobile phones.
  • Promoted to IPS in Dec 2012, SP Raebareli from Feb 12 20o13 to 8 June 2014.
